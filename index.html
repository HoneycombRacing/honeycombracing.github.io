what is F1 in Schools?

A STEM Competetion

Schools Ltd is a social enterprise working with committed industry partners to provide an exciting yet challenging educational experience through the magnetic appeal of Formula 1.


Helpng Youth get skills

The challenge inspires students to use IT to learn about physics, aerodynamics, design, manufacture, A-set_later-, graphics, sponsorship, marketing, leadership/teamwork, media skills and financial strategy, and apply them in a practical, imaginative, competitive and exciting way.




Is it a charity?

yes.The founding constitution of F1 in Schools stipulates that it is, and shall remain, a not-for-profit organisation. Funds raised through sponsorship are invested in administering, developing and expanding the challenge.

2022 - Regionals

After a year of hard work, we competed in the 2022 Scottish Regional Finals, where we were crowned Regional Champions. We earned awards for Best Engineered Car and Fastest Car.

Our Regionals Car

Our regional’s car was a product of long hours spent designing, manufacturing, and testing. From the aerodynamic vortex generators to the hybrid bearings of our SLS printed wheels, this car brought to the table a plethora of innovations which won us the ‘Best Engineered Car’ and ‘Fastest Car’ awards.

Nationals

As we approach the National Finals in January, we’ve been working tirelessly to raise the standard of our work to compete with the best teams from around the UK. Our engineers are hard at work developing our car designs, as they consider new approaches and methods to improve our car’s performance, while our enterprise team liaise with our sponsors and consolidate our brand presence. The team is dedicated to putting our best foot – or wheel – forward as we roll on towards victory.
